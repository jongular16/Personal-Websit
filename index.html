The Impact of Prompt Quality on Learning

This research explores the role of prompts as pedagogical tools, where well-designed prompts guide LLMs to provide deeper reasoning and insight, while poor construction leads to shallow or misleading outcomes.

Key points:

  • Methodology: A series of controlled experiments compares four prompt types—open-ended, scaffold, instructional, and reflective—to measure their impact on learning outcomes.
  • Metrics: Evaluation is based on response accuracy, conceptual depth, learner interaction, and post-interaction comprehension.
  • Significance: The study provides designers and educators with a framework for integrating learning science with prompt engineering, treating prompting as an essential educational design strategy.
  • Goal: To optimize human-AI dialogue for better cognitive benefits, critical thinking, and responsible AI use in education.

AI-Powered Detection of Dental Conditions

This project proposes an automated detection pipeline for analyzing dental panoramic X-rays using a YOLOv8 object detection model trained on a dataset of 9,000+ annotated images. My team and I implemented the interface, preprocessing, and postprocessing components, and integrated the model into a complete Flask-based web application. The system produces annotated images and condition summaries, showcasing experience in ML engineering, model evaluation, and real-world inference integration.

TechTeacher Platform Overview

TechTeacher is an AI-powered educational platform that transforms complex, dense topics into concise, visually engaging, and easy-to-understand learning materials. Designed to reduce cognitive overload for students and lifelong learners, the platform leverages AI to provide structured summaries and clear explanations that accelerate concept mastery. Operating on a scalable, subscription-based model, TechTeacher aims to revolutionize the learning experience by making high-quality knowledge more accessible, with future plans to integrate interactive animations and enhanced AI capabilities.
